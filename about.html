About Us

Sign2Text is the research project made in two term sequence by the final year students of university of newcastle. The purpose of the application is to communicate with deaf people who use american sign langugage.


What Buttons do

Test

Test allows you to train data set pictures hence makes the better accuracy output.

Translate

Translate helps you to distinguish what sign has been shown on particular period.

Learn

Learn helps application to capture the images from users to train models.
