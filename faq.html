Frequently Asked questions


How accurate are the predictions?

The accuracy might vary depending on lighting and background noise, but the trained model returned 97% accuracy on our test data.

Why does sign2text use ASL?

ASL is the most commonly used form of sign language in the world. Using ASL makes Sign2Text more accessible in general. It also only requires 1 hand which makes mobile usage much easier.

My readings aren't very accurate. Can I improve this?

For best results, try using a solid background with good lighting and make sure your hand is contained within the blue box.

I don't think my camera is working

Make sure your browser allows use of the webcam. If so, there should be a popup asking for permission when the page loads. Just click "allow".

Does it work on mobile and desktop?

Yes. Sign2Text will work both on mobile and desktop, provided it has access to a camera.

How does it work?

Sign2Text uses a convolutional neural network trained on thousands of hand images to make predictions of what gesture is being shown.

What about privacy? Does it save my images?

Sign2Text knows nothing about the person using it. Once the image has been processed to make a prediction, it is discarded and never used again. Nobody else has access to it.
